Non Sequitur

Random quote from someone on your team

Use /quote to get a blast from the past

Remember that one time when someone talked about potatoes? Quote it with /quote potatoes.

Or just quote a completely random thing someone said a while back with /quote @user.

Why??

Because it's funny!

Open source

If you'd rather host this yourself or you just want to peek under the hook, go ahead and check out the source code for Non Sequitur.

Permissions

Here are the permissions that Non Sequitur needs and why:

  • Confirm your identity: get the Slack ID for the user adding this app
  • Add commands: Add /quote to your Slack team
  • Search your team’s content: to find something to reply to /quote
